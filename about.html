Quality Changes The World

At Poclane, we are dedicated to offering innovative and reliable construction machinery solutions to meet the evolving needs of the infrastructure and heavy equipment industry. With years of expertise and a commitment to excellence, we provide a diverse range of high-performance machinery designed to support a variety of construction, mining, and material handling operations. Our state-of-the-art equipment is built using cutting-edge technology, ensuring exceptional reliability, fuel efficiency, and increased productivity. We prioritize customer satisfaction and operational efficiency, offering not only world-class products but also excellent after-sales services and support. Poclane is your trusted partner for construction machinery, combining performance, quality, and customer-centric solutions to build a strong foundation for your success. Whether you're working in urban development, mining, or road construction, we are committed to delivering machinery that helps you achieve your project goals with efficiency and precision.

World-Class Quality: POCLANE equipment meets international standards, offering durability and resilience suitable for the toughest construction environments. Japanese-Powered Engines: Our imported engines provide superior performance and longevity, making them a dependable choice for heavy-duty operations. Enhanced Digging Depth: POCLANE’s mini excavators offer the highest digging depth in their respective categories, delivering exceptional value and efficiency to users. Competitive Pricing: We offer world-class machines at reasonable prices, making advanced construction technology accessible. Comprehensive Service and Support: With a dedicated service team, we ensure prompt maintenance, minimizing downtime for customers. Extended Warranty & Parts Kit: All machines come with an extended warranty and a one-year filter kit, reducing maintenance costs and enhancing equipment lifespan. Sales Goals Our ambitious target includes selling 10 machines per month in the next 3 months, aiming for a consistent increase to 50 machines per month within three years. With a focused approach to customer satisfaction and quality, POCLANE is set to become a leading name in construction equipment across India.
